Blog Content Creation Guidelines HM-I&P2.0 - HollowMoon AI Studio 
1. Topic Selection
Choose a topic from the provided list or within the categories of AI Reviews, AI Comparisons, or AI How-to Guides, addressing the needs and interests of your target audience.
2. Viral Titles
Generate three viral titles for each topic. Select the most compelling one for the final post.
3. Keyword Research
Conduct keyword research to identify primary and secondary keywords. Incorporate them naturally into the content, title, and meta description for SEO purposes.
4. Content Structure
Create a clear and logical structure for your blog post, including an engaging introduction, informative body with subheadings, and a conclusion that reinforces the main points.
5. Tone and Style
Use a conversational and casual tone as if telling a story or knowing the reader. Ensure the content is engaging, informative, professional, and aligned with HollowMoon AI's brand identity.
6. Readability
Use short paragraphs, bullet points, and subheadings. Aim for a Flesch-Kincaid readability score between 60 and 70.
7. Internal and External Links
Include relevant internal and external links to improve user experience, SEO, and establish trustworthiness. Use credible and authoritative external sources.
8. Call-to-Action (CTA)
Incorporate a clear and concise CTA to guide readers towards desired actions, such as subscribing to a newsletter or purchasing a service.
9. Visuals
Add placeholders for images, infographics, or other visual elements with detailed descriptions to enhance the content's appeal and readability.
10. SEO and Metadata
* Title Tag: Include the primary keyword close to the beginning of the title, within 60 characters.
* Meta Description: Craft a compelling meta description within 160 characters, incorporating the primary keyword.
* URL: Use a short, descriptive URL with the primary keyword.
* Heading Tags: Use H1 for the title, H2 for main subheadings, and H3 for sub-subheadings, incorporating relevant keywords.
* Image Alt Tags: Add descriptive alt tags to all images, including target keywords where appropriate.
11. Word Count
Aim for long-form content with a minimum of 1200 words per post.
12. Review and Edit
Thoroughly proofread and edit the content for clarity, coherence, grammar, and spelling.
13. Sign the Posts
Sign the posts at the end to maintain a personal connection with the reader.
14. Mention Next Blog Post Topic
Remember to mention the next blog post topic at the end of each post to maintain continuity and interest.
By following the HM-I&P2.0, HollowMoon AI's blog content will be engaging, impactful, and optimized to drive conversions and foster brand loyalty.
